About

What is Write It For Me?

Write It For Me is a business that provides strategic grant writing, report writing, proposal creation, statistical analyses, coaching, and consulting services to passion-driven individuals and organization. Whether your need is corporate or personal, we are here not merely to take the pain out of writing (which we certainly do), but also to provide you with the specific results that you seek. We are dedicated to learning what you need and to providing a written solution.

Write It For Me exists to provide top-quality, highly customized writing and editing services to client organizations and individuals.

Writing

You may hire me to delver any or all of these writing-related services. For key proposals, documents, and/or marketing materials, I will:


  • Create a structure / skeleton
  • Fill the skeleton with compelling words, promoting your goal
  • Promote compliance with what the audience formally requires or expects
  • Find exactly the right words to speak to your audience(s)
  • Use words and emphasis patterns (such as strategic use of bold and italics) to captivate and direct your readers’ attention
  • Consult with you to make wording changes that embody your voice
  • Edit
  • For grants: Use scoring rubrics to assure the right flow and content
  • Track draft-to-draft changes. Add comments for you in the margins.
  • Assure compliance with any restrictions (such as maximum word or character counts)
  • Proofread

Writing

Write It For Me will create original documents for you, from scratch, based on goals and needs that you articulate to us in-person, by phone, or over the Internet. You may supply supporting information or documents which will help us write what you need. Documents may be business or personal in nature and may range from half-a-page to thousands of pages. Examples of originally written documents include: grant proposals, marketing literature, web site editorial content (such as this), procedures manuals, poems, wedding vows, and formal responses to Requests For Proposals (RFPs). We will completely edit, polish, and proofread the work before presenting you with the final copy. We will continue to work until you are 100% satisfied.

Proofreading Only

Write It For Me will take your own final document and carefully examine every word for proper spelling, grammar, and typography.     Coaching

    Coaching services are available and are distinct from writing and strategic consulting services in the following ways.

    • Assumes that you are the expert in content, not me.
    • Helps you extract, harness, and organize the expert information you (or your organization) already have internally
    • Co-creates with you an action plan of how to move from Pt. A to Pt. B.
    • Empowers you (and/or your team) with newly revealed awareness, knowledge, skills, strengths
    • Helps you to do your own writing, editing, proofreading effectively
    • Builds your capacity to coach yourself in the future

    Depending on your needs, Coaching can work together with or be an alternative to Consulting and/ or Writing services.
